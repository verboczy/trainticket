CATEGORIES
type(I): cash; credit card
cash(F): payment success; payment failure
credit card(F): valid credit card; invalid credit card

output(O): success; failure


CONSTRAINTS
cash succes: WHEN type IS cash AND cash IS payment success AND credit card IS SKIPPED THEN output IS success

cash failure: WHEN type  IS cash AND cash IS payment failure AND credit card IS SKIPPED THEN output IS failure 

credit card succes: WHEN type IS credit card AND credit card IS valid credit card AND cash IS SKIPPED THEN output IS success 

credit card failure: WHEN type IS credit card AND credit card IS invalid credit card AND cash IS SKIPPED THEN output IS failure
